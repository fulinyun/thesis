Hello! This is the main page for PROV-PUB-O, the specialized provenance ontology for research publications, developed by Linyun Fu.

PROV-PUB-O is modularized into two parts:

  • PROV-PUB-O/S for a publication structure model, and
  • PROV-PUB-O/P for a result generating process model. (Currently under construction so not available now.)
